About Me

A peek into my little world

Woof! Woof! Hi there, this is Mika! I am a bordercollie–poodle mix, this tiny fluffball was me when I was just a baby. I am 4 years old now!

Going to the off-leash park is my absolute favorite! And sometimes I pretend I don’t hear my human calling me because I’m having way too much fun. Hehe.

Ball, ball, ball! My favorite thing ever! I can toss it around by myself, but playing with humans is the BEST. I like the “try to take it from me” game, and I always win!

My Skills
